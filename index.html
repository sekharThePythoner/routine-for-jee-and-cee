Important Study Tips

1

Lecture ke baad same topic MCQ

Concept fresh hota hai. Isi time practice karne se topic strong hota hai.

2

5-minute skip rule

Ek question pe 5 min se zyada stuck hua toh mark karo aur next question. Warna pura slot barbaad.

3

Error notebook banao

Har galti ka reason likho: concept mistake, calculation mistake, formula bhool gaya, silly mistake.

4

Sunday analysis serious lo

Test dena important hai, lekin analysis usse bhi important hai.

5

Coding ko reward ki tarah use karo

Coding dream hai, isliye chhodna nahi. Bas strict 1.5 hr limit maintain karo.

Mistakes Jo Avoid Karni Hai

Lecture binge

Sirf lecture dekhne se selection nahi hota. Practice zaroori hai.

11th ignore

JEE me 11th ka base strong nahi hua toh 12th bhi weak lagega.

No sleep

Sleep kam hua toh focus, memory aur speed teeno down honge.

Hard question trap

Ek tough question pe 20 min waste mat karo. Mark and move.

Random YouTube

Study ke naam pe random video mat dekhna. PW + module + PYQ enough.

Backlog snowball

Pending ka list banao. Sunday ko clear karo.
